The most east House of the village is a simple hut which seemed to house one light spirit.
Inside, hidden among a lot of other pages a journal can be found. The entries aren't interesting,
except the latest one, where the light spirit wrote:
"I don't know what's happening, we have all been feeling a bit weird for some days but then some off us stopped glowing, growing darker and darker and getting more aggresive. Last night i heard crashing noices from another house, i'm not sure i should go out today."
